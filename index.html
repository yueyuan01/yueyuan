Welcome to My Website.

Yue (Wilson) Yuan is a doctoral candidate in the Department of Criminal Justice at Indiana University Bloomington. His research interests include juvenile delinquency, victimization, and perceptions of crime. He received his Mater of Statistics in 2014 with specialization in SEM, GLM, GLMM, Social Network, and Causal Analysis. Wilson uses Stata and R most frequently. He is also comfortable with MLwiN, SPSS, MPlus, SAS, and Python.
